How to Use This Directory

This directory is designed to help you explore and choose from master thesis projects. Each project includes a title, detailed description, and relevant keywords to help you find projects matching your interests.

  • Browse by Title — Scroll through the project list to see titles and keywords.
  • Search & Filter — Use the search bar to filter by title or keyword, or click keyword tags to narrow results.
  • Detailed View — Click on any project card to expand the full project description and details.
How to Apply
  1. Review the full project descriptions and requirements carefully. Then, select up to five (5) projects that interest you the most.
  2. Fill out the application form. Ensure that you rank your choices in order of preference. Please note that the order matters — R1, R2, R3 is different from R3, R2, R1 in terms of your priority.
  3. Project allocation will be based on students' preferences and supervisor availability. As a result, you may not be assigned your top choice. To improve your chances of a good match, we recommend selecting a diverse set of projects that genuinely interest you, rather than concentrating only on a few popular options.
Application Deadline: 15th April 2026
